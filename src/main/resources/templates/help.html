Basics

  • When you accessing articles, url will look something like "..../newsarticle/8". You can navigate to articles using the index at the end of the url. (in the example index is 8)
  • Once in the article you can see comments listed in the table below the article body. Each comment has 4 radio buttons.
  • Select one of the 4 options as necessary for each comment.
  • Once you are done with the article hit save button at the bottom of the page. It will not save if you forget to hit save before navigating out.
    • Please contact Isuru: theisuru@gmail.com
  • To freely navigate without saving use next or previous buttons.

Sentiment

  • Skim the news article to get an idea about what it is.
  • Check if the comment is negative or positive against the news article. i.e. commenter agrees or disagrees with the article or globally
  • Tick appropriate radio button.
  • If the comment has mixed sentiment, tick conflict.
  • If the comment has no sentiment, tick neutral.
